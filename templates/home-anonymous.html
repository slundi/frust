Features

  • Atom and RSS support.
  • Save the article you like or download it.
  • Filter the content you want or don't want. You can also use regex if you are familiar with it.
  • No tracking, Frust is free and open source. You can host it on your own server
  • Frust is light and fast: it is written in RUST and use a SQLite database. It is not designed to support a heavy traffic... but it is a database limitation.
